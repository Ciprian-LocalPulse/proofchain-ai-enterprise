# ProofChain AI Enterprise

Enterprise proof infrastructure for AI-generated business assets.

This repository is the public evaluation surface for ProofChain AI Enterprise: a proprietary, acquisition-ready digital asset designed for organizations that need verifiable provenance, tamper-evident records, and executive-grade confidence around AI-generated or workflow-generated business outputs.

The public repository is intentionally limited. It is designed for external review, buyer orientation, brand validation, and procurement-safe due diligence. It does not expose private source logic, internal prompts, customer data, secrets, commercial strategy, deployment credentials, or proprietary implementation details.

## Current Positioning

ProofChain AI Enterprise is positioned as a trust and provenance layer for the AI economy. It is built around a simple enterprise problem:

Organizations increasingly create contracts, reports, media assets, compliance outputs, code artifacts, and strategic documents with AI-assisted workflows, but they still need defensible proof of origin, integrity, approval, and timestamped existence.

ProofChain AI Enterprise addresses this through a private enterprise system concept covering:

- digital asset fingerprinting
- timestamped proof records
- verification workflows
- evidence-grade certificates
- audit-oriented asset history
- enterprise presentation and commercialization material
